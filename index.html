Our Story

Obsessive Attention to Detail.

We don't just fix cars; we restore confidence. Situated in the heart of Cacora, Goa, Torque Automotive represents the pinnacle of modern car servicing.

With a state-of-the-art facility and specialized master technicians, we treat every vehicle—from daily drivers to high-performance exotics—with uncompromising precision. We blur the line between a clinic and a garage.
